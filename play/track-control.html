How to Play Track Control



Simple Controls, Endless Challenge



In Track Control, your goal is to move the ball safely across a set of suspended platforms. The platforms may rotate, shift, or shrink, making every bounce a thrilling moment. Tap or click with perfect timing to keep the ball balanced. Land at the center of the platform to stay secure and prepare for your next jump. It's a true test of hand-eye coordination and patience!



Tips for Success




  • Stay Centered: Always aim for the middle of the platform to maintain stability.

  • Watch Platform Movement: Some platforms rotate or move – adjust your timing carefully.

  • Plan Ahead: Look ahead to the next platform and prepare your move in advance.

  • Stay Calm: Rushing leads to mistakes. Keep your movements steady and deliberate.
