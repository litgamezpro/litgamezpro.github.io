How to Play Shade Shuffle



To master Shade Shuffle, players must:


  • Drag and drop tiles of similar shades into matching columns or rows.

  • Complete matches by aligning three or more of the same shade.

  • Beat the timer to advance through increasingly difficult levels.

  • Use hints or boosts when you're stuck on a tough combination.
