How to Play Cubes Got Moves



In Cubes Got Moves, your objective is to slide colorful cubes across the grid to create matches of three or more cubes of the same color. The game board is filled with cubes in different hues, and your task is to align them in horizontal or vertical rows to make them disappear. When you create a match, the cubes vanish, making way for new ones to fall in their place.



As you progress through the levels, the game becomes more challenging with limited moves and tricky cube placements. You will need to plan your moves carefully and use your strategy to clear the board or achieve specific goals within a limited number of moves. Each level offers a new puzzle to solve, testing your skills and making the game more thrilling with every passing challenge.
