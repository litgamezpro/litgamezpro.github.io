🛫 What is Flying School?



Flying School is more than just a free flying game for kids. It’s a full-fledged casual flight simulator game with simple yet realistic controls. The game places you in an animated aviation environment where you complete missions, avoid aerial obstacles, and land with precision. It teaches you the basics of piloting in a fun and interactive way.



🎮 How to Play




  • Arrow Keys – Steer the aircraft left, right, up, and down

  • Spacebar – Control altitude and initiate landings

  • Avoid obstacles such as clouds, birds, and buildings

  • Land safely on designated runways to earn points



✈️ Key Features




  • Flight simulator-style controls simplified for all ages

  • Colorful, cartoon-like visuals perfect for younger players

  • Cross-device compatibility – play on mobile, tablet, or PC

  • Educational value – introduces basic flight mechanics

  • No downloads or installs – play instantly online
