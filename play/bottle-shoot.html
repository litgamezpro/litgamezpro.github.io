Overview



In Bottle Shoot, the objective is simple yet exhilarating—shoot and break as many bottles as you can with limited ammo. The game features a variety of bottle types that move unpredictably, rotate, and fall, making your shots more difficult to land. As you advance through the stages, the difficulty increases with faster movements and more obstacles. It's a true test of timing, accuracy, and patience. With clean 3D graphics and immersive sound effects, the game ensures an enjoyable experience for all age groups.


How to Play



  • Aim your weapon using your mouse or touchscreen.

  • Tap or click to shoot the moving bottles before they vanish.

  • Watch out for red bottles – hitting them will reduce your score!


Key Features



  • Realistic 3D Graphics: Enjoy detailed bottle textures and physics-based movement.

  • Progressive Difficulty: Levels become more challenging, requiring faster reflexes.

  • Responsive Controls: Smooth aiming and shooting mechanics for better precision.
