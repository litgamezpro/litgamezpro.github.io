How to Play?

The goal is to sort the water so that each bottle contains only one color. Follow these easy steps:

  • Tap a bottle to pick it up.
  • Tap another bottle to pour the water into it.
  • You can only pour if the colors match and there is enough space.
  • Repeat until all bottles are sorted correctly!
