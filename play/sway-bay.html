How to Play Sway Bay



Playing Sway Bay is easy to understand but tough to master, making it perfect for both beginners and experienced players alike. The objective is to swing your character from tower to tower using a rope without falling into the water below. Each successful swing earns you points, and the challenge increases the farther you go.




  • Click and hold to extend your rope to the desired length.

  • Release the click to swing toward the next platform.

  • Time your release for maximum accuracy—too short or too long, and you'll fall!

  • If your character misses the platform, the game resets and your progress is lost.



This gameplay loop is simple but endlessly entertaining, making every session unique and competitive. Whether you're going for a personal best or just trying to beat your friend's high score, the fun never stops.
