Set in a beautifully animated, moonlit world, Witch Flight takes you through a series of dynamic levels filled with obstacles, spooky enemies, and collectible magic orbs. As you guide your witch across floating platforms and starry skies, you’ll need sharp reflexes, clever maneuvers, and magical upgrades to succeed.

How to Play Witch Flight



  • Use arrow keys or touch controls to steer your witch’s broomstick.

  • Avoid oncoming obstacles like ghosts, bats, and magical barriers.

  • Collect glowing orbs to increase your score and unlock power-ups.

  • Use magical spells to clear paths and survive longer in each flight.
