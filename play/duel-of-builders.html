Duel of Builders – Free Online Fighting Game at LitGamez


Duel of Builders is a pixel-style action game where chaos unfolds on a construction site. Workers from different crews clash in a wild brawl using wrenches as their weapon of choice. Whether you play solo or battle a friend in two-player mode, the goal is clear—defeat your rivals and survive the site!


Choose Your Mode: 1-Player or 2-Player


At the start of Duel of Builders, you can pick between playing alone or facing off with a friend in two-player games. Each player takes control of a team of construction workers. The match begins with fast movement and quicker attacks. Use smart timing and reflexes to dominate the battlefield.


Gameplay Features



  • Classic pixel-style brawler design

  • Wrenches drop from the sky—grab them fast

  • Each hit lowers your opponent’s health bar

  • Multiple weapon options

  • Instant action and responsive controls


Weapons play a huge role in this dueling game. You can switch tools mid-battle to find the best way to take down your rival. But don’t relax—your opponent can do the same and turn the tables quickly. Health bars show who’s on top, and the last team standing takes the win.
