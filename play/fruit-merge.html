The Origins of the Game


Fruit Merge is inspired by the popular Japanese game Suika. Suika explores physics with fruit rolling and settling in a container and merging fruits, but rather than being a fast paced puzzle game, Suika is more about slow, strategic gameplay focusing on balance and fruit combinations. Fruit Merge Game Online uses this combination of slower, but challenging gameplay to implement its own simple swiping and always evolving fruit, that can be fun for gamers of all ages.



How to play Fruit Merge:


This game is about merging the same fruit to create newer, higher-level fruits. As you merge fruits in the same type level, they will continue to evolve into even higher-level fruits, until your goal of getting the Golden Watermelon.


You will use your finger and swipe across the screen to merge two identical fruits together, for example, two lemons will merge into a coconut. In this Merge Fruits Puzzle Game, you can merge over 200 fruits.



General Guidelines:


- Combine two fruits that are equally to merge.


- Every fruit pairs will create a fruit one level above what was merged.


- Each time you merge will increase the rarity and value of fruits.


- The game is about using your brain to think and plan, and moving with a purpose when you play. The key to the entire game is limited board space. When the board fills with fruits that can not be merged the game is over. Your goal in the game is to keep the board as cleared of fruits as possible and build the biggest fruit.



Fruit Merge Tips and Winning Strategies:


- Plan out every move, so you do not run out of space


- Once you merge a fruit, keep building higher level fruits rather than merging them even if they are lower level fruits


- Work to keep space available so that you create opportunities for higher-level fruit to create larger combinations.


- Don't forget to find rare fruits to unlock hidden gifts in the Maximal fruit merge game.
