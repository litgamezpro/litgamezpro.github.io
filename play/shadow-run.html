Shadow Run: Cyberpunk Fantasy Awaits


Step into the dark and gritty world of Shadow Run, a thrilling cyberpunk fantasy RPG set in the Sixth World. In this dystopian future, advanced technology and ancient magic collide, creating a high-stakes environment where mercenaries known as shadowrunners are hired for dangerous, covert operations. Navigate through neon-lit megacities, corporate intrigue, and powerful mystical forces in a tactical, turn-based strategy game that combines deep storytelling with immersive gameplay.



How to Play: Your Journey as a Shadowrunner



In Shadow Run, you take on the role of a shadowrunner, a mercenary for hire who must navigate a world of mega-corporations, underground gangs, and hidden magical factions. Here’s how to dive into the action:


Create Your Shadowrunner


Choose from a variety of unique classes, such as the cybernetically enhanced Street Samurai, the tech-savvy Decker, or the powerful Magical Mage. Customize your abilities, cybernetic enhancements, and magical powers to suit your personal playstyle. Whether you prefer close combat, hacking systems, or casting spells, the choice is yours to create a character that fits your vision.



Explore the World


Immerse yourself in the neon-lit, dystopian world of the Sixth World. As you explore, interact with NPCs, gather intel, and complete missions. Every action you take and every decision you make will affect the world around you, making each playthrough unique. Form alliances, navigate dangerous environments, and face the challenges that come with being a shadowrunner.


Engage in Tactical Combat


The game features turn-based combat, where you’ll need to use strategy and careful planning to defeat your enemies. From choosing the best position on the battlefield to utilizing your character’s unique abilities, combat in Shadow Run requires a mix of tactical thinking and real-time decision-making. Take advantage of cover, plan your moves, and lead your team to victory.



Make Story-Driven Choices


Every decision you make matters. Whether you choose to work with a corporation, align with magical factions, or betray allies for personal gain, your actions will impact the narrative and determine the outcome of your journey. Each mission offers multiple paths and outcomes, ensuring replayability and a rich, dynamic experience.



Key Features of Shadow Run




  • Tactical Combat: Engage in turn-based strategy combat combined with real-time decisions to plan your moves and execute strategies.

  • Character Customization: Build a shadowrunner with cybernetic upgrades, magical powers, and specialized skills tailored to your playstyle.

  • Rich World-Building: Explore a deeply immersive world filled with corporate espionage, powerful megacorporations, and magical forces. The Sixth World is filled with lore and exciting narrative twists.

  • Story-Driven Choices: Make impactful decisions that shape your journey, with branching storylines offering different outcomes based on your actions.



Why Play Shadow Run?


Shadow Run is the perfect fusion of cyberpunk technology and fantasy magic. Whether you prefer high-tech warfare or casting powerful spells, Shadow Run offers an immersive and engaging experience with rich storytelling and dynamic gameplay. Every decision you make influences the story, giving you the opportunity to explore different paths and outcomes in each playthrough.



With its tactical combat system, deep customization options, and a rich, immersive world, Shadow Run offers hours of replayability. Dive into the shadows of the Sixth World and shape your destiny as you face the challenges of this dark and dangerous future.



Ready to take on the world of Shadow Run? Join the ranks of elite shadowrunners and carve your path through the dystopian streets where magic and machine collide.
