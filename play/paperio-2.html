How to Play Paper.IO 2


Simple Controls, Deep Strategy


In Paper.IO 2, you control a colorful block that glides across the map. Use your mouse or keyboard to navigate and capture territory. To claim new areas, draw a line outside your existing territory and complete the loop by connecting back to your zone. Once the loop is closed, the new area becomes part of your empire!


But beware — while you are outside your area, your trail is exposed. If another player crosses your line, you’re eliminated! Timing, quick reflexes, and smart strategy are essential for survival.


Top Tips for Mastering Paper.IO 2


Play Smart, Stay Ahead


1. Start Small: Expand your territory little by little to avoid being an easy target.


2. Watch Your Tail: Always keep an eye on your trail to prevent sneak attacks.


3. Strike Opportunistically: Attack other players when their tails are exposed to quickly eliminate the competition.


4. Use the Edges: Moving along the borders of the map provides natural protection and better control over your territory.
