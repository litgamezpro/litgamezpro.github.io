How to Play Slit Sight


In Slit Sight, your mission is to guide your character through a series of shifting walls and narrow slits. You must tap at the perfect moment to slide through moving gaps without colliding with the barriers. The game starts off easy but quickly ramps up in difficulty, requiring sharper focus and faster decision-making with every level.


Each wall has a unique pattern, and predicting movement is key. As the speed increases, you’ll need to rely on precision, reflexes, and calm under pressure to survive. The simple one-tap control scheme makes it accessible for all ages but tough to master for anyone looking to top the leaderboards.
