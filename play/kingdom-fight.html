Game Overview


In Kingdom Fight, you take command of a besieged kingdom and must protect your castle from waves of invading monsters and warriors. With each level, the pressure increases, demanding smarter decisions and faster reflexes.


This strategy game blends intense action with thoughtful planning. Armed with a variety of magical and mechanical weapons—including arrows, electric bolts, fire catapults, and time warpers—you’ll fight to hold the line. Every battle is unique, with shifting enemy formations and evolving tactics required to stay alive.


How to Play

  • Use time warpers to slow enemies and regain control of the battlefield
