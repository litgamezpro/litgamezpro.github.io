How to Play High or Low



In High or Low, the gameplay is easy to pick up, but the challenge increases as you progress. You start with a card and must guess whether the next card will be higher or lower. With each correct guess, you earn points and move forward, but one wrong guess means the end of the game. The key to mastering the game is quick thinking and trusting your instincts as the pace picks up. Each round presents new opportunities to score big, but be careful—wrong guesses can bring your game to an end!
