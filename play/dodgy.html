How to Play Dodgy



In Dodgy, your mission is to dodge incoming obstacles from every direction. It starts off slow, but as you advance, the speed and pattern of obstacles increase rapidly. You control a small object in the center of the screen using one-touch controls. Your job is to maneuver around the screen to avoid being hit. The longer you survive, the higher your score.



Precision and split-second decision-making are critical. With obstacles appearing unexpectedly, players must stay alert and adapt quickly. The minimalist design ensures no distractions—just you and your reflexes against the challenge.
