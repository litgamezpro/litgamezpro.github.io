Game Overview



OneBit Adventure is a retro-inspired RPG where players must survive against waves of increasingly powerful enemies. Choose from multiple character classes, each offering unique abilities to suit your style. As you progress, you’ll uncover hidden treasures, level up attributes, and unlock new strategies to overcome tougher foes. The game's pixel-art design and simple yet deep mechanics make it accessible and engaging for all types of players.

How to Play



Navigate the world using WASD or arrow keys, or simply hold the left mouse button to move and attack automatically. Activate powerful skills with Alpha Keys 1-7 to turn the tide of battle. Each step forward presents a new threat or treasure, testing your strategic thinking and survival instincts. Master your chosen class and push deeper into the perilous unknown!
