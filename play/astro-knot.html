Game Overview



In Astro Knot, you take control of a space explorer lost in a galaxy filled with danger. Your mission is to jump, dodge, and maneuver your way through a variety of cosmic levels filled with laser traps, moving platforms, and gravity-defying zones. Thanks to the game's HTML5 technology, it plays seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones, giving you the freedom to enjoy the action wherever you are.



The game's intuitive controls, paired with vibrant space-themed visuals and futuristic sound effects, create an immersive and engaging environment. Each level introduces new obstacles, ensuring a fresh experience that rewards reflexes, timing, and smart decisions.



How to Play



  • Use arrow keys to move the astronaut left or right.

  • Press the spacebar to jump across platforms and avoid gaps.

  • Dodge lasers and falling obstacles to reach the exit safely.

  • Survive as long as possible while collecting stars or other in-game boosts.
